* The purpose of this directory is to give us a
  layout of the different user experiences the user
  may encounter.

- Example subdirectories include; AdminLoggedIn,
  UserLoggedIn, AdminLoggedOut, and UserLoggedOut.

- Things in and nested within this directory will
  most likely have global implications. Things seen
  and used by most users.

Sample Directory Tree:

|-- layouts
    |-- LoggedIn
        |-- LoggedIn.js
        |-- index.js
        |-- styles.js
    |-- LoggedOut
        |-- LoggedOut.js
        |-- index.js
        |-- styles.js
